Admissions Tip of the Day

Tip: Tailor your activities list descriptions to reflect key qualities colleges seek. Highlight leadership, impact, initiative, and teamwork.

Action Steps:

  • Review your current activities list descriptions.
  • Identify the 2-3 most impactful achievements for each activity.
  • Rewrite descriptions to emphasize leadership and measurable impact.

Example:

"Led a team of 10 volunteers to organize a fundraising event that raised $2,500 for local charities."
